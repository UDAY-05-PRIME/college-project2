About Us

Our Story

Parakeet Cafe sprouted from one of the greatest challenges in our lives. It emerged from a deep-rooted desire to heal and grow, both as individuals and as a community. Our journey began with our daughter Michelle, who was born with a rare disease that required chemotherapy when she was just four months old. Faced with this immense challenge, we couldn't simply sit still and wait for her healing; we had to take action. Our quest for solutions led us to a remarkable discovery: food as medicine. Food became the cornerstone of Michelle's healing journey, mending her on a small scale and infusing us with resilience. It was our way of giving back to the community that had supported us on our path. Parakeet Cafe became the vessel for this purpose. It's a place where people can explore the incredible potential of food, organic ingredients, embrace the vibrant colors of the rainbow on their plates, and share these experiences with loved ones with Love, Carol and Jonathan Goldwasser President and CEO
